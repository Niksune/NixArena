How to play NixArena?

Log in to your account or create your account and access your account by navigating through the navigation bar.

Create your first character to fight every hour.
You can create up to 3 characters.

In the account menu, you can assign weapons obtained through combat to your characters.
You can also watch the past fights of your characters.
